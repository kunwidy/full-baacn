Asia Africa City Network

In order to strengthen the position of Bandung City as a center for collaboration and networking between cities in Asia and Africa, the Bandung City Government through the Regional Development Planning Research and Innovation Agency (Bapperida) initiated the formation of the Asia Africa City Network (AACN) as a platform for cooperation between Asian African cities in the fields of economy, culture, education, technology, and sustainable urban development.

  • Establishing communication and initial commitments between cities in Asia and Africa
  • Conducting technical discussions regarding the structure, governance, and initial work programs of the AACN
  • Building a global city network based on the spirit of Asian-African solidarity
